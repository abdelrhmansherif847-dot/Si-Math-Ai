  • 1 SAT Question = 1 Credit
  • 1 Question Image = 1 Credit
  • 10 Questions = 10 Credits
  • PDF uploads consume credits based on the number of analyzed questions

Frequently Asked Questions
Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from Settings. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing cycle.

Do unused credits roll over?

Credits are valid for the duration of your billing cycle. Monthly credits reset each month; quarterly and annual credits are allocated upfront for the full period. Unused credits do not carry over after a cycle ends.

Will I lose my history if I downgrade?

No. Your learning history is always saved. On the Free plan you'll just have limited access to older records, but nothing is deleted.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade to Pro whenever you're ready, and your progress carries over instantly.

What payment methods are supported?

We support Visa, Mastercard, Meeza, Apple Pay, InstaPay, and Vodafone Cash — all securely processed through Paymob, Egypt's trusted payment gateway.
